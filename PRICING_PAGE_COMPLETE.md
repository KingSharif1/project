## 💼 Your Sales Flow

### Current Process (Perfect!)
1. **Customer sees pricing page** → Understands what you offer
2. **Customer contacts you** (email or phone from pricing page)
3. **You discuss their needs** → Determine which plan fits
4. **You create pending signup** in Company Signups tab
   - Enter their info
   - Select the tier (Basic/Premium/Enterprise)
5. **You send payment link** → They pay for the plan you chose
6. **Webhook auto-creates account** → They receive login credentials
7. **Customer starts using the system**

### Why This Works
- **Pricing page** = Professional first impression
- **Contact sales** = You control the conversation
- **You choose plan** = Based on their actual needs
- **Payment link** = Locked to the right tier
- **Automatic setup** = No manual work
